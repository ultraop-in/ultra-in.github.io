Tic Tac Toe

About Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe (also known as Noughts and Crosses or Xs and Os) is a classic paper-and-pencil game for two players, X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3Ã—3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game.

Play Tic Tac Toe

How to Play

  • Click on any empty cell to place your mark (X or O).
  • Player X goes first.
  • The game ends when one player gets three of their marks in a row, or when all nine squares are filled.
  • Click "Reset Game" to start a new round.
  • Choose between Player vs Player or Player vs Computer modes.
