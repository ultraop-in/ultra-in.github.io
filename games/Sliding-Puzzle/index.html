Sliding Puzzle (15 Puzzle)

About Sliding Puzzle

The 15 Puzzle is a classic sliding puzzle game consisting of numbered tiles in a 4Ã—4 grid with one missing tile. The goal is to arrange the tiles in numerical order by sliding them into the empty space. It's a perfect brain teaser that challenges your logical thinking!

How to Play

  • Click on a tile adjacent to the empty space to move it
  • Arrange the numbered tiles from 1 to 15 in order
  • The empty space should be in the bottom-right corner
  • Use the minimum number of moves possible
  • Track your time and try to beat your best record
  • Use the shuffle button to start a new puzzle
  • Use the solve button if you get stuck
  • Green tiles indicate correct positions
