Snake Game

About Snake Game

Snake is a classic arcade game where you control a growing snake to eat food while avoiding collisions. The snake gets longer with each food consumed, making the game progressively more challenging. Test your reflexes and strategic thinking!

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or WASD to control the snake direction
  • On mobile, use the on-screen directional controls
  • Eat the red food to grow your snake and increase score
  • Avoid hitting the walls or the snake's own body
  • The snake moves faster as your score increases
  • Use space bar to pause/resume the game
  • Press 'R' to restart the game
  • Try to beat your high score!
