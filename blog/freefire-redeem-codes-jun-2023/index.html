How to redeem

  1. Open the official Rewards Redemption Site in your browser.
  2. Login with the same account you use in-game.
  3. Paste the 12-character code and submit.
  4. Rewards arrive in your in-game mail if valid and unexpired.
Never share OTP or passwords. Avoid third-party sites that ask for login.
